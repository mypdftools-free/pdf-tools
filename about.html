About Us

My PDF Tools is a free, open-source collection of browser-based PDF utilities. Our mission is to provide fast, private, and easy-to-use PDF tools without requiring any file uploads.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Built with modern web technologies including pdf-lib, jsPDF, and PDF.js.
